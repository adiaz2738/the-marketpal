About The MarketPal

Hi, I’m Anthony, the creator of The MarketPal. This website was meant to help investors and those who want to learn to invest, find a place they can go to in order to start their research, find learning tools, and ideas instead of going all over the internet looking for it.
That’s not to say that you shouldn’t always find your own sources and do your own research. But it’s always nice to have a place to start, especially if you’re just starting out.


Who I Am

My name is Anthony and I’m just someone who enjoys talking all about the stock market. I dedicate a lot of time to it and I get a huge sense of satisfaction and enjoyment about investing, It’s something I’m very passionate about and although I’m not a financial advisor by any means, I’m always learning and improving alongside those who follow what I do. Investing is a very interesting and amazing thing and what isn’t there to love about it.
