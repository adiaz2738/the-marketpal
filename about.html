About The MarketPal

The MarketPal is a website that gives investors a place to start their stock research. The MarketPal has a public portfolio that can be seen by users and each new addition will have a stock analysis journal to go along with it so users know why that stock was added to the portfolio. On top of that, there will be new research papers every week so that investors can find the stocks they're looking for on the website as well.
On the website, users are also allowed to suggest or submit their own research papers on stocks that interest them for a small fee if they would like to support the website. This fee ranges from $5-10 depending wether it's a suggestion or a submission of their own.
In addition to these services, there is also a YouTube channel and podcast that will have videos uploaded to it every week. These videos will be on the topic of the stocks in the portfolio, the research papers, and any stocks in the market in general that are worth talking about.
